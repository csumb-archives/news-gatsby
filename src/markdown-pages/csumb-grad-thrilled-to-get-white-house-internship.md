<p>Zoë Carter of Los Olivos expected hard work and long hours as an intern at the White House, and that’s what she got. Despite the time commitment of the unpaid position, the 2010 CSU Monterey Bay graduate said her four-month-long stint at 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue is the best and most exhausting thing she’s ever done.  – <em>Santa Ynez Valley News</em>, Jan. 19, 2012
